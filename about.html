Our Story

A legacy of textile excellence

Rooted in Tradition

Velvet Fabrics was founded in 1992 with a simple mission: to bring the world's finest fabrics to discerning creators. What began as a small shop in Brooklyn has grown into a trusted partner for apparel makers, interior designers, and luxury brands worldwide.

Our founder, Elena Vasquez, spent years traveling through Europe and Asia, building relationships with artisanal mills and family-owned weaving operations. Those relationships continue to form the backbone of our collection today.

We believe that exceptional textiles are the foundation of exceptional creations. Every bolt in our collection has been personally selected for quality, character, and craftsmanship.

Our Process

From fiber to fabric

01

Sourcing

We travel the world to discover exceptional materials—organic cotton farms in Turkey, silk houses in Italy, wool mills in Scotland. Every partnership begins with a personal relationship.

02

Selection

Each fabric undergoes rigorous evaluation for hand, drape, durability, and beauty. We test washability, colorfastness, and wear characteristics before adding anything to our collection.

03

Curation

Our collection is intentionally edited to offer only the finest options. We organize by fiber, weight, and application to help you find exactly what you need.

04

Service

From swatch requests to custom orders, our team provides personalized support. We understand that fabric selection is crucial to your creative process.

Our Commitment

We are committed to preserving the art of textile craftsmanship while embracing innovation and sustainability. Every fabric we offer meets our exacting standards for quality, ethics, and environmental responsibility.
